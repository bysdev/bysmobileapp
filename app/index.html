askBYS.app

The askBYS.app is a mobile extension of the askBYS.org web site. It provides for quick access to askBYS resources, including:

- Questions posted be peers and answered by Bainbridge Youth Services therapist
- Forms for asking questions to BYS therapists, local Bainbridge doctors and making BYS appointments.
- Access to askbys FaceBook and Twitter pages, which host more resurces on youth mental health topics

askBYS is a service of Bainbridge Youth Services, a fifty year old organization whose mission is to promote the social and emotional well being of adolescents and young adults on Bainbridge Island, WA.
